[THE_LEARNING_JOURNEY_BEGINS]

Around October of 2024, I decided that I wanted to learn computer architecture. For one, I was very interested in how some configuration of switches can eventually become a computer. I also wanted to learn computer architecture such that I was prepared for my computer organization course.

[BUILDING_A_STUDY_GROUP]

So I gathered my friends and did some advertising on campus and got 16 people interested in studying this thing with me. Pretty cool.

[THE_NAND2TETRIS_APPROACH]

I've decided that we would read the NAND2Tetris book, otherwise known as Elements of Computing Systems, and simulate our logic as the main method of troubleshooting. And then we would have weekly labs to just socialize on the material and play around with some electronics.

[WHAT_WE_BUILT]

We made adders, memory like RAM and ROM, an arithmetic logic unit (ALU), a central processing unit (CPU), and then a full computer.
